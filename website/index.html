Sparky the Tadpole

An autonomous vortex-shedding swimmer.


Sparky is an exploration of fish swimming patterns, and the actuation and parameters required to optimize them. Ultimately, Sparky is servo-actuated for vortex-shedding, and coded to swim autonomously at different amplitude, phase offset, and frequencies optimized for forward velocity.
