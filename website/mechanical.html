Mechanical System

Our final mechanical system involves a body constructed of foam, acrylic, metal ballast, and carbon fiber sheet whith three actuated joints, overall designed to shed vortices in water.

Actuation

To make our fish go forward, we chose a propelling method in which swimming surfaces wiggle to push against water. The three swimming surfaces attached to fish head wiggle according to our code and thus move the fish to any direction.

Prototyping

As far as getting the fish being able to float on water is concerned, we had to balance the weights of each section so that it does not tilt to one side.
